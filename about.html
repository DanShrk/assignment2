About Me Daniel Sharkey

Bio

My name is Daniel Sharkey, I am Creative Digital Media student Currently attending The Institute Of Technology Blanchardstown. I am interested in Photography, Design and Illustration. On this portfolio website, you will find examples of my work over the first two semesters of my first year of college. This includes photography work, Illustration/design work, media and video work, and others. I hope you enjoy your visit


My key skills include my creativity, aptitude for visual communication and my willingness to learn and work as a team to reach a goal. I enjoy working on projects that include visual elements, such as advertisement or video. I hope to have a career in some sort of creative industry.
